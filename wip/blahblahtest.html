Description

#

Borin Wheatflow is a 23 year old male human pickpocket.

#

He has very long, wavy, auburn hair and golden eyes.

#

He has soft, sunburned, red skin.

#

He stands 172cm (5'7") tall and has a round build.

#

He has a round, mediocre face.

#

#

He is branded as a slave on his right hand.

#

#

Personality Traits

#

He quietly worships Akadi, Goddess of air, movement and speed. (True Neutral)

#

He is always prepared.

#

He is very quick to trust other people.

He cares about his friends and will do anything for them.
